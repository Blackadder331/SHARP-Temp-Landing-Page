Sight Unseen


Sight Unseen:

Providing empathetic design outreach for our vital community orgs reaching the invisible and underserved.

In 2024 we’re committing to make a small difference where we can: Northwest Arkansas. We want to support our local partners in new ways and help them more effectively reach the people they serve.

To initiate, we're donating six hours of service to six vital organizations. And if you’re feeling charitable, you can join us in this effort. For every purchase of our brand launch merchandise we'll add three hours of design services to a chosen local partner from our list of six. We'll reveal the total hours donated on January 31st on our website and we’ll begin applying these contributions in February. Thank you for supporting our inaugural Sight Unseen season!
