Sight Unseen

Providing empathetic design outreach for our vital community orgs reaching the invisible and underserved.

About the initiative

In 2024 we’re committing to make a small difference where we can: Northwest Arkansas. We want to support our local partners in new ways and help them more effectively reach the people they serve.


Here's how it works:

  1. We're donating six hours of design and development service to six vital community organizations
  2. For every purchase of our brand launch merchandise, we'll add three hours of design services to a chosen local partner
  3. We'll reveal the total hours donated on February 29th before applying these contributions in March

Meet the partners

Ozark Circle for Choice

We are a secular collective of Northwest Arkansas residents who believe in the right to reproductive and abortion services free from judgment or stigma. We provide support for any pregnant person regardless of their beliefs or situation. We value mutual aid and envision a community in which we collaboratively meet each other’s needs. We believe that no one should have to justify their needs in order to have them met.

Arkansas Justice Reform Coalition

AJRC is dedicated to ending mass incarceration, led by those directly impacted. They advocate for a community centered on safety, harm prevention, and incarceration alternatives. Their work involves collaborative partnerships, system agitation, and education about mass incarceration's impacts. They aim to build safer, more inclusive communities by forming strong coalitions and envision a world where incarceration is rare and individuals can thrive.

Donate

One Family Outreach

Founded in memory of those who have lost their lives to opioid overdose, they seek to aid others on their road to recovery. One Family Outreach is a 501(c)3 nonprofit that funds the distribution of life-saving naloxone opioid overdose reversal kits and other harm reduction services while working to break down the stigma and encourage hope, healing and dignity in care.

Donate

Arkansas Crisis Center

The Arkansas Crisis Center is a nonprofit organization focused on suicide prevention, providing immediate help and long-term hope for those in need. The organization aims to restore hope, empower people, and save lives through its helpline services. They offer confidential and anonymous intervention, counseling, and resource linkage. Their ultimate goal is to achieve zero suicides in Arkansas by 2025.

Donate

Welcome to Kessler Mountain

Welcome to Kessler Mountain, a fundraising initiative to create a new entrance for the Fayetteville Traverse Trail system. Established in 2015, the 380-acre Kessler Mountain Reserve was protected through conservation efforts and partnerships between the Fayetteville Natural Heritage Association, the City of Fayetteville, and the Northwest Arkansas Land Trust. This conservation easement safeguards over 10 miles of trails for hiking and mountain biking, along with vital wildlife habitats.

Learn more

Blue Dog Farm

Blue Dog Farm in Wesley, Arkansas, is not just a tree farm and nursery; it's a local business committed to environmental sustainability. By cultivating a wide variety of trees and plants, Blue Dog Farm plays a crucial role in absorbing carbon dioxide, a key factor in addressing climate change. Blue Dog Farm demonstrates how local enterprises can positively impact the environment and contribute to the preservation of our natural ecosystems.
